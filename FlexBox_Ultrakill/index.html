V1 is the character

which the player takes the

role of in ULTRAKILL,

a blood-fuelled machine

descending into Hell for fuel

the extinction of mankind.

V1 does not initially

possess any weapons of its

own beyond the Feedbacker,

but gradually obtains

a diverse arsenal of

weaponry throughout its

descent into Hell.

V1 has a thinner,

more fragile chassis.

However, this makes

it capable of

repairing itself via direct

contact with fresh blood.
